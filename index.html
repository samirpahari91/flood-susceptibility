Area analysis

polygon summary

Use a drawn or uploaded polygon to summarize the total area in each of the five susceptibility classes. The underlying raster stores continuous probability values from 0 to 1, classified into Very Low, Low, Moderate, High, and Very High at equal intervals of 0.2. Area is reported in square kilometers.

Upload a zipped shapefile containing the watershed polygon files together, or draw directly on the map. AOI summaries are computed client-side from the full probability raster.

Implementation notes

important

Polygon analysis sends the drawn boundary to TiTiler for server-side raster statistics. Results typically return within seconds with no large downloads.

Area totals use a 5 ft cell resolution. The continuous 0-1 probability surface is classified into five equal-interval classes (Very Low through Very High) using breaks at 0.2, 0.4, 0.6, and 0.8.

Upload support accepts .zip shapefiles or GeoJSON. For shapefiles, package the .shp, .dbf, .shx, and .prj files together in one ZIP archive.
